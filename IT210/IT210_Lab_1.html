IT210 Lab 1 9/4/2003
In todays lab we are going to set-up our client computers for our class.  We will be running XP Pro on the clients.  For each step listed below you need to get approval by the instructor before you move onto the following step.  Please work in pairs of 2 on the lab, although you each will install the operating system on your own clients.  I would suggest you pick some one sitting next to you.  The computer you set-up today as your client will remain your computer for the rest of the semester.  If you need additional help with the lab I have included appropriate web sites to guide you along the way.  


1.  Identify the Hardware, software and media necessary for your network.  Please write this information down. Additionally provide a document of what your network will look like.
